People First Design

A design philosophy that puts real people at the centre of every decision — not trends, metrics, or egos.

What is People First Design?

People First Design is a philosophy and practice that prioritises the needs, comfort, and dignity of the people using a product or service — above all else. It means building things that are accessible, readable, fast, and honest. It means removing friction, respecting attention, and never manipulating or deceiving users.

The core idea is simple: every design decision should benefit the person using it, not just the person building it.

This design system was originally created by COOLmanYT.

Our Commitment

This site is built with People First Design principles in mind. That means:

  • Accessible markup with proper semantic HTML, ARIA labels, and skip links.
  • No dark patterns — no deceptive UI, fake urgency, or manipulative design.
  • Privacy-respecting analytics (no advertising trackers).
  • Fast, lightweight pages — no unnecessary bloat.
  • Clear, honest language throughout, including on legal pages.
  • Readable typography with sufficient contrast and spacing.

This is an ongoing commitment, not a one-time checkbox. Feedback is always welcome via the contact page.
